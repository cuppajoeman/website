--- title: trick tips ---

general

  • keep the board under you
  • try to float above the board for as long as possible
  • shoulders paralell to board
  • warm up

kickflip

  • foot position
    • ollie position with front foot a little more heel side
    • keep back foot a little heel side (counteract any front side rotation)
  • balance
    • shoulders directly parallel with the board, try not to do any front side rotation, imagine doing a backside 180 right before popping to counteract any front side rotation
  • flick
    • ollie first modify the foot position on the way up
    • flick on the way up, flick the board upward

heelflip

  • foot position
    • back foot in middle of tail, parallel with trucks front foot up by bolts hanging off top of toe
  • balance
    • balance in center of board, don't lean toeside
  • flick
    • up the board and toside off the edge of the nose, try to drag 1/2 of the side of your shoe up it
