--- title: trick tips ---

general

  • keep the board under you
  • try to float above the board for as long as possible
  • shoulders paralell to board
  • warm up

kickflip

  • foot position
    • ollie position with front foot a little more heel side
  • balance
    • shoulders directly parallel with the board, try not to do any front side rotation, think of a backside 180 to counteract
  • flick
    • ollie first modify the foot position on the way up
    • flick on the way up, flick the board upward

heelflip

  • foot position
    • back foot in middle of tail, front foot up by bolts hanging off top of toe
  • balance
    • balance in center of board, don't lean toeside
  • flick
    • up the board and toeside
