--- title: trick tips ---

general

  • keep the board under you
  • try to float above the board for as long as possible
  • warm up

kickflip

  • foot position
    • ollie position with front foot a little more heel side
    • keep back foot a little heel side (counteract any front side rotation)
  • balance
    • shoulders directly parallel with the board, try not to do any front side rotation
    • imagine doing a backside 180 right before popping
  • flick
    • flick on the way up, flick the board upward
